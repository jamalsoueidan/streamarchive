# Stream Archive

**[Stream Archive](https://www.streamarchive.io)** is a cloud-based live stream recorder that automatically captures and saves live broadcasts from your favourite creators — so you never miss a moment.

## What It Does

Live streams are temporary. Once they end, they're gone forever. Stream Archive solves this by recording streams automatically in the cloud, 24/7. No software to install, no screen recorder needed, no computer left running.

Just add the creators you want to follow and every live session is captured and saved to your personal stream library.

## Supported Platforms

- **TikTok** — Record TikTok Lives before they disappear
- **Twitch** — Download Twitch VODs and save streams
- **YouTube** — Capture YouTube Live streams automatically
- **Bigo** — Record Bigo Live streams in the cloud
- **Tango** — Save Tango live broadcasts
- **Kick** — Record Kick streams automatically
- **AfreecaTV** — Download AfreecaTV streams
- **Pandalive** — Record Pandalive broadcasts

## Features

- **Automatic recording** — Streams are captured the moment a creator goes live
- **Cloud-based** — No software, no extensions, works on any device
- **Built-in player** — Watch saved streams directly in your browser
- **Video downloads** — Download full recordings to your device (Premium)
- **Multi-platform** — All your creators in one library, regardless of platform

## Getting Started

1. Visit **[streamarchive.io](https://www.streamarchive.io)**
2. Create a free account
3. Add your favourite creators
4. Streams are recorded automatically whenever they go live
